Gobble Gums ®
E X E C U T I V E   S U M M A R Y

 'Gobble Gums' company will offer healthy, flavorful, and affordable chewing gum products containing xylitol, a
 unique sugar alternative and a low-calorie sweetener that prevents plaque buildup, giving every customer a cleaner
 smile. Our sugar-free gum products are perfectly made to satisfy children who are prone to tooth decay, diabetic
 adults, and all sweet-craving individuals who are into diet and fitness. Research shows that the majority of gum
  products in the Philippines do not contain xylitol. Moreover, historical data shows that about 92.4% of Filipinos have
 tooth decay and suffer from dental problems. An advocate from ‘Smile Pilipinas’ also once emphasized that oral
 health is simply not a priority in the Philippines. With this, producing gum products that will potentially be highly
 beneficial for Filipinos’ oral health will also serve as an important starting point; a way to spread awareness of how
 important oral care is. Our primary strategy is to introduce the health benefits our gum products can offer by
 highlighting the use of xylitol, our product's unique ingredient, Our business will initially operate home-based and use
 social media (Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram) to promote our chewing gum products. The gum making will be
 made in a household. The materials required are sanitized, and the ingredients needed are purchased in advance from
 various stores. For a customer to place an order for our gum products (in pre-order), he/she must get in reach with us
 via our Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter accounts. They could select their preferred gum flavor and type of packaging
 from our shortlist. As of now, we offer 5 various flavors and 3 different types of packaging (per pack). Each chewing
 gum piece includes different motivational quotes in its packaging (per piece). Only orders per pack will be accepted.
 When the gums are finished, they will be delivered directly to the customer's home. Once the target market is reached,
 gobble gum will be available at small dental clinics in Pasig City. Overall, our gum products have a number of
 competitive features that will be used to their advantage to achieve market penetration such as the health benefits it
 can offer, added unique features, and affordability. The initial capitalization for the business, good for a monthly
 expense, is ₱35,691.18, Expected revenue for Year 1 is ₱76,545.84, and the total cost of goods sold amounted to
 ₱504,840.
