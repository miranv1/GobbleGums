 SALES STRATEGY
 Due to the Covid-19 Pandemic, communicating with potential consumers to spread the existence of the product is not possible. As a
 result, digital marketing will be used to increase the visibility of Gobble Gums as a sugar-free chewing gum for all sweet-loving
 individuals.
 
  • Social Media - The company will create a Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram account dedicated to creating posts to introduce, inform, and advertise the product to people, particularly people with a sweet tooth.
 
  • Website - The company has an active website filled with information about the company and its chewy gums. In addition, updates and recent news will be posted on the website, alongside the updates from the social media platforms mentioned earlier.
