 SALES STRATEGY
 To ensure the ordering of the Gobble Gums will be simple and accessible, the company will use Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram as
 tools for ordering for the consumers. As a result, consumers must contact us via our social media accounts to place an order for the
 products.
 
 Gobble Gums will also use four factors to boost its sales. Firstly, the packaging of the product must be eye-pleasing and make the
 consumers excited to start chewing the gums. Secondly, the price needs to be affordable and reasonable so that customers feel
 relaxed and ready to buy Gobble Gums. The third is perfect customer service, which convinces the buyers to view the company as a
 professional seller with genuine care for its customers. Lastly, sales promotion will bring different offers to excite the consumers.
